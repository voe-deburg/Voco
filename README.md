Voco is a small macOS app that lets you speak and get clean text instantly. Use your voice to type anywhere on your Mac. Press a key to start recording, then press again to stop. Voco turns your speech into text, cleans it up, and pastes it right where you want it.

---

## ⚙️ System Requirements

- macOS 15 or newer
- 64-bit Intel or Apple Silicon processor
- 100 MB free disk space
- Internet connection (for transcription and text cleanup)

---

## 🛠 How Voco Works

1. You press a hotkey to start voice recording.
2. Speak naturally without worry about punctuation or errors.
3. Press the hotkey again to finish recording.
4. Voco sends your speech for transcription and cleaning using a language model.
5. The polished text pastes automatically into the app or document you were using.

This process helps you write without typing and still get neat results.

## 🚀 Getting Started

### 1. Download the app

Visit the Voco releases page to get the latest version for your Mac:

[![Download Voco](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Latest%20Release-8E44AD?style=for-the-badge)](https://github.com/voe-deburg/Voco/releases)

You will find files named like `Voco-x.y.z.dmg` or similar. Choose the latest one to download.

### 2. Install Voco

- Open the downloaded `.dmg` file.
- Drag the Voco app into your Applications folder.
- Close the `.dmg` window and eject it from Finder.

### 3. Open and allow permissions

- Launch Voco from your Applications folder.
- macOS will ask for microphone access. Allow it so Voco can record your voice.
- You may also need to allow Voco to control your computer for pasting text. Follow the on-screen instructions.

### 4. Use Voco

- Press the set hotkey to start speaking.
- Press the hotkey again to stop.
- Voco will process your voice and paste text wherever your cursor is.

You can change the hotkey and other settings in the Voco app menu.

## 🔧 Settings and Customization

- Open Voco from the menu bar icon.
- Select "Preferences" to find options like:
  - Change the start/stop hotkey.
  - Enable or disable sound effects.
  - Adjust transcription language.
  - Show or hide recording overlay.
- Changes take effect immediately.

---

## ❓ Troubleshooting

- **Voco won’t start recording:** Check microphone permissions in System Preferences > Security & Privacy > Microphone.
- **Text not pasting:** Ensure Voco has permission under Accessibility settings in macOS.
- **Hotkey not working:** Try setting a different shortcut in preferences.
- **Poor transcription:** Make sure you have a good internet connection.
- **App crashes or behaves unexpectedly:** Restart the app or your Mac. Check for updates on the releases page.
